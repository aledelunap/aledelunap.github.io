Building a Climbing Wall

During a pandemic of an airborne virus, such as COVID-19, it is a smart move to avoid close and crowdy spaces, like climbing gyms. Thus, with the help from my family, I decided to build a small climbing wall in our garage. I share some images of the process, wich, in retrospect, required more work in stages I didn't anticipate would be so laborious.
